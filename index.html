MY JOURNEY SO FAR!

Where it all began.

It started in the quiet town of Cawayan, Masbate, Philippines. Where i grow up and finished my high school education. Then i moved to Cebu City, Philippines to finished my college degree in Bachelor of Science in Commerce major in Business Administration. During college days i worked part time as a service crew in a fastfood chain and after college been exposed to different fields like, sales, marketing, franchising and Business Development.

Philippines is an island country in Southeast Asia with abundant marine biodiversity, picturesque landscapes, worldclass beaches, and the best roast pig ever tasted by Anthony Bourdain, just to name a few.

Talking about beaches, the island of Cebu boast a number of beaches that everyone can enjoy, from backpackers budget to 5 star(‑) accommodations.

I miss the beach but i am loving my stay here in St. Louis, Missouri. It's my new home and this is an exciting adventure as i move on with the journey of my life.
